A Little Intro to Count Dooku

Count Dooku was not always a Sith. He was a Jedi at one point and was trained by Yoda. His real name isn't Dooku, but is actually Tyranus. Later down the road, he found some things that he didn't like and killed a Jedi Master named Yaddle who is believed by a few Star Wars ™ fans is Yaddle was Yoda's wife and they had Grogu together, so that's why Yoda doesn't like Dooku. After killing Yaddle, he left the order and then Palpatine came to him with a deal no one could resist, so he joined the Sith at that point. No one knows when that was, but with the shows and movies, we can take a guess that he didnt start doing things with the Sith until Attack of the Clones ™, meaning he couldv'e turned to the Sith between Episode 1 and Episode 2 or before Episode 1. Nobody really knows, but the fandom can theorize about it until they inevitably make a show or movie on Count Dooku.
